Europeana: razmislite o kulturi

Započnite virtualno putovanje kroz građu koju možete naći na Europeani.

Explore millions of items from a range of Europe's leading galleries, libraries, archives and museums. Books and manuscripts, photos and paintings, television and film, sculpture and crafts, diaries and maps, sheet music and recordings, they’re all here. No need to travel the continent, either physically or virtually!

Found something you like? Download it, print it, use it, save it, share it, play with it, love it!

Europeana is the trusted source of cultural heritage brought to you by the Europeana Foundation and a large number of European cultural institutions, projects and partners. It’s a real piece of team work.

U više od milijun jedinica na Europeani mogu se pronaći ideje i inspiracija. Te su jedinice:

  • Slike - slike, crteži, karte, fotografije i slike muzejskih predmeta
  • Tekstovi - knjige, novine, pisma, dnevnici i arhivski dokumenti
  • Zvukovi - glazba i izgovorena riječ iz cilindara, traka, diskova i radijskih emisije
  • Video - filmovi, filmski žurnali i TV emisije

Some items and topics are world famous, like Isaac Newton's book about the Laws of Motion, crteži Leonarda da Vincija, Johannes Vermeer's painting of the Girl With A Pearl Earring ili objekti o Berlin Wall. Drugi su skrivena blaga, koje čekaju da ih otkrijete.

Renowned names such as the British Library in London, the Rijksmuseum in Amsterdam and the Louvre in Paris are featured alongside smaller organisations across Europe. Together, their assembled collections allow you to explore Europe's history from ancient times to the modern day.

Whether you find a celebrated work or a lesser known object, Europeana always connects you to the original source of the material so you can be sure of its authenticity.

For more information, you can visit Europeana Professional, our knowledge sharing platform.
