Europeana - Termos e Políticas

In this section of the Europeana website you will find an overview of our Terms and Policies. These are divided into three subsections: 'Using Our data', 'Contributing data' and 'Our Policies':

Using Our data provides you with the Terms of Use and Guidelines for using the data published on Europeana.eu.

Termos de uso governs what you can or cannot do with the metadata and previews that are published on Europeana.eu, with seperate Terms of Use for the Europeana API. Europeana publishes guidelines for working with Europeana metadatos as well as for using digital reproductions of Obras de dominio público que se atopan a través de Europeana Additionally we provide a list of fontes de datos que usa Europeana to enrich the metadata provided by our data providers.

Colaboradores de datos provides terms that ensure that Europeana can combine objects and data provided by individual users with the rest of the Europeana metadata.

Acordo de Intercambio de Datos de Europeana governs the relationship between Europeana and the institutional data providers. The termos de contribucións de usuarios governs the terms on which users contribute data to Europeana.

As nosas políticas contains Europeana's policies.

The Privacidade policy details how we treat personal information that we have collected from our users. The Idioma policy describes Europeana's policy for providing information in a users own language and Accesibilidade describes Europeana's aim to provide functionality and content that is universally accessible. In this section you will also find the Carta de Dominio Público de Europeana que explica a contribución de Europeana a fortalecer o dominio público dixital.
