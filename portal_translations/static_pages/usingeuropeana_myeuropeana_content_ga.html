Using My Europeana

Sábháil chuig My Europeana is your personal space on Europeana.

Register for Sábháil chuig My Europeana and you can:

  • Sábháil an cuardach seo: Bíodh an t-eolas is úire agat mar gheall ar thopaic in Europeana trí chuardach a shábháil, i dtreo is go mbeidh sé éasca duit an cuardach céanna a dhéanamh sa todhchaí.
  • Cuir clib leis seo: Label your favourite items in Europeana with tags that mean something to you. You can then see all objects you have tagged, sorted by the keywords you have chosen, in the My Europeana interface.
  • Sábháil chuig My Europeana: Stóráil na míreanna is fearr leat chun féachaint orthu níos déanaí.
  • API keys: If you are a developer who has registered for one or more API keys, these will be shown on the API Keys tab.

To save a search, add a tag or save an item, simply login to Sábháil chuig My Europeana and click on the relevant action from the menu.

To view your saved items, searches and tags, use the Sábháil chuig My Europeana shortcuts in the header.
