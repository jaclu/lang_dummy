Europeana: think culture

Ħu vjaġġ virtwali madwar it-tip ta' affarijiet li tista' ssib fuq Europeana.

Explore millions of items from a range of Europe's leading galleries, libraries, archives and museums. Books and manuscripts, photos and paintings, television and film, sculpture and crafts, diaries and maps, sheet music and recordings, they’re all here. No need to travel the continent, either physically or virtually!

Found something you like? Download it, print it, use it, save it, share it, play with it, love it!

Europeana is the trusted source of cultural heritage brought to you by the Europeana Foundation and a large number of European cultural institutions, projects and partners. It’s a real piece of team work.

Ideas and inspiration can be found within the millions of items on Europeana. These objects include:

  • Xbihat - pitturi, tpinġijiet, mapep, ritratti u stampi ta' oġġetti minn mużewijiet
  • Testi - kotba, gazzetti, ittri, djarji u materjal minn arkivji
  • Ħsejjes - mużika u kelma mitkellma irrekordjati fuq ċilindri, tejps, diski u xandiriet tar-radju
  • Vidjows - films, films t'aħbarijiet u xandir tat-TV

Xi punti u suġġetti huma magħrufin mad-dinja kollha, bħall-ktieb ta' Isaac Newton dwar il- Liġijiet tal-Moviment, id-disinnji ta' Leonardo da Vinci, il-pittura ta' Johannes Vermeer Tfajla Tal-Misluta Bil-Perla jew oġġetti dwar il- Ħajt ta' Berlin. Oħrajn huma teżori misturin, jistennewk biex tiskoprihom.

Renowned names such as the British Library f'Londra, ir- Rijksmuseum f'Amsterdam u l- Louvre f'Pariġi jidhru maġenb organizzazzjonijiet iżgħar madwar l-Ewropa. Dawn il-kollezzjonijiet, miġmugħa flimkien, iħalluk tesplora l-istorja tal-Ewropa miż-żminijiet tal-qedem sal-ġurnata tal-lum.

Sew jekk issib xi xogħol ċelebri, sew jekk xi xogħol inqas magħruf, l-Europeana dejjem se tgħaqqdek mas-sors oriġinali tat-tali materjal sabiex int tkun ċert mill-awtentiċità tiegħu.
