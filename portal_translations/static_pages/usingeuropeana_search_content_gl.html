Busca xeral

Searching the millions of items in Europeana is simple. Just enter your search term and hit Busca.

Busca específica

You can narrow your search with the arrow to the right of Search All fields and selecting a category: Titles, Creators, Subjects, Dates/Periods, Places. Then enter your search term and hit Busca.

Alternatively, just ask yourself who, what, where or when you are interested in and type these words into Europeana's search box followed by a colon (:). Then enter your search term and hit Busca.

For specific searches, you could:

  • Use the Creador filter or enter who: followed by a name of, for example, an actor, author or architect. Your results will display only items for which the name you entered is listed as the creator. For example, searching for who: Vincent van Gogh afinará os seus resultados ás obras de van Gogh, e excluiranse fotografías do artista ou documentais sobre el e a súa obra.
  • Use the Materia ou Título filter or enter what: followed by a type of item, a subject you are interested in or words from the title of the item you are looking for. Your search results will display only items of the requested type or subject or items whose title includes your search term. For example, searching for what: maps mapas afinará os seus resultados a mapas, ou what: Art Nouveau Modernismo mostrará obras de arte, edificios e monumentos modernistas.
  • Use the Place filter or enter where: followed by a name of a town, city or country within Europe or around the world. Your search results will display only items which were created in, published in or depict the place you are searching for. For example, searching for where: Milan will help you differentiate your search when you are looking only for objects created or held in Milan, excluding items on writer Milan Kundera from your search results.
  • Use the Data filter or enter when: followed by a date (e.g. 1945) or a period (e.g. Roman or Medieval). Your search results will display only items for which the date is listed in the item's date field. For example, searching for when: 1984 will help you differentiate your search when you are looking only for items dated 1984, excluding items that have 1984 in their title or description, like George Orwell's novel.

These search functions also have support for different spellings and languages. For example, when you search for where: Milan, os resultados tamén incluirán ítems con Milano, Mailand ouro Milanas dado que figuran como alcance xeográfico.

Busca por frase

Busca por frase tamén é posible. Poñer comiñas (e.g. “art nouveau”) ao redor da frase que desexa buscar, ou escriba AND entre as palabras crave (ex. art AND nouveau).

Excluír palabras escribindo NOT en maiúsculas, seguido da palabra que se quere omitir (ex. Auguste NOT Renoir)

Another helpful search feature is Ordes. Start typing a search term. Europeana will suggest words that may match what you are thinking of. Just select the term you want and hit Busca

Afinar a súa busca

 

Unha vez faga unha procura inicial, pode reducir os resultados coa función afinar a procura Afinar a súa busca functions. These are in the grey pane on the search results screen. You can add more keywords to your search, thereby narrowing the search results. Just type your keyword into the Add more keywords field and hit + Engadir. The keyword is displayed at the top of the pane, under Matches for.

To remove a keyword, hit the X icon next to the relevant keyword in the Resultados para list.

Tamén pode filter your search results:

  • Polo tipo de medio - will display only objects of a selected media type (images, text, video, sound or 3D).
  • Polo idioma - will display only objects with descriptions in selected languages, i.e. the language of the institution that provided the object to Europeana.
  • Pola data - will display only objects with selected dates.
  • Polo país - will display only objects from selected countries, i.e. the country of the institution that provided the object to Europeana.
  • Por dereitos de autor – will display objects labelled with the selected copyright label(s). This copyright label indicates how you may reuse the digital object, including the preview.
  • Polo provedor - amosar só obxectos de institucións seleccionadas

Select the filter name, e.g. 'By language' to expand it. Then just use the check boxes for the categories you wish the search results to include. The results will refresh automatically. The filter will be added to the Resultados para list at the top of the pane.

To remove a filter, hit the X icon next to the item in the Resultados para list, or de-select the check box next to the filter category.

You can also select to Incluír contido aportado polo usuario. This means items that have not come from a cultural institution but from an individual user, for example, items contributed to the Europeana 1914-1918 project.

Share your search with friends by choosing Compartir. A full list of social media sharing options is presented. Just select one you want. This also includes print and email options.
