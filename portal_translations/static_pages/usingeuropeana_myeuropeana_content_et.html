Using My Europeana

Minu Europeana is your personal space on Europeana.

Register for Minu Europeana and you can:

  • Salvestada otsinguid: Hoidke ennast kursis konkreetse teemaga Europeana vahendusel ja salvestage oma otsing, et saaksite tulevikus seda hõlpsalt korrata.
  • Lisada märksõnu: Label your favourite items in Europeana with tags that mean something to you. You can then see all objects you have tagged, sorted by the keywords you have chosen, in the My Europeana interface.
  • Salvestada Minu Europeanasse: Salvestage oma lemmikud edaspidiseks vaatamiseks.
  • API keys: If you are a developer who has registered for one or more API keys, these will be shown on the API Keys tab.

To save a search, add a tag or save an item, simply login to Minu Europeana and click on the relevant action from the menu.

To view your saved items, searches and tags, use the Minu Europeana shortcuts in the header.
